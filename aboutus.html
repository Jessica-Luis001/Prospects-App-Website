About The Creators

A little about me

This website and app was created by five high school girls who came together to create an app to help ourselves and our peers.
We were determined to find a solution to common problems that many high school student have such as finding internships and dressing for job interviews. As part of the Girls Who Code Immersion Program, we worked together to create a place where we can find all the information about college and the future for high school students.
